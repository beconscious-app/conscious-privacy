Privacy Policy

Conscious · Last updated: June 9, 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how the Conscious app ("the app," "we," "us") handles your information. Our approach is simple: your data stays on your device, and we do not collect it.

Information We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or store any of your personal or financial information on our servers. The app has no servers. Everything you enter — transactions, budgets, categories, and settings — is stored locally on your device only.

How Your Data Is Stored

All information you create in the app is saved on your device using the device's local storage. This data never leaves your phone, is not sent to us or any third party, and is not backed up to our systems. We have no ability to access, view, or recover it.

Third-Party Services

The app does not integrate any third-party analytics, advertising, tracking, or bank-connection services at this time. The app makes no network requests to transmit your data.

Data Sharing

Because we do not collect your data, we do not and cannot sell, share, or disclose it to anyone.

Data Deletion

You are in full control of your data. You can delete individual entries within the app at any time. Deleting the app from your device permanently removes all data stored by the app.

Children's Privacy

The app is not directed to children under 13, and we do not knowingly collect information from anyone. Because all data stays on the device and is never transmitted to us, no personal information is gathered from any user.

Changes to This Policy

If the app's features change in a way that affects how data is handled — for example, if optional bank-connection features are added in the future — this policy will be updated, and the "Last updated" date above will reflect the change. Any future features that involve transmitting data would be optional and clearly disclosed before use.

Contact
